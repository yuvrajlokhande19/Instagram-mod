About Our Firm

Founded in 1990 by Adv. A.V. Linpane, Linpane Legal Associates has established itself as one of Nagpur's most respected law firms. With our main office strategically located in the legal district of Nagpur, we have successfully represented clients in landmark cases at the Nagpur High Court.

Our team combines traditional legal wisdom with modern approaches, ensuring comprehensive solutions for our clients. We understand the complexities of the Indian legal system and provide personalized attention to every case.

With over 1000+ cases successfully resolved and a client satisfaction rate of 98%, we pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence and our track record of success across various legal domains.
