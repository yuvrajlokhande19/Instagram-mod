About Our Firm

Established in 2010 by Adv. Ashish V. Linpane, Linpane Legal Services has grown to become one of Wardha's most respected and trusted law firms. With our main office in Wardha , we serve clients across Maharashtra and nationwide.

Our firm combines traditional legal values with modern approaches to deliver outstanding results. We understand that legal matters can be stressful, and our team is committed to providing compassionate, personalized service while aggressively protecting your rights.

We have successfully handled over Various Numbers Of cases in Nagpur High Court And Wardha District & Session Court and various District Courts and maximum client satisfaction speaks volumes about our dedication and expertise.
